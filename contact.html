Contact Us

The café is located on County Hall Westminster Bridge Road, Riverside Buildings, London SE1 7PB. Nearest Tube Station access is via Westminster station providing fast access via the Jubilee and Circle lines. The Café is off the bank and located close to the scenic Westminster Bridge. For any queries or suggestions please do not hesitate to contact via telephone on 0203 6451 236. Please refer to our opening hours for appropriate times to call in to the café. If contact via email address is preferred, please contact us on npccafes@npc.com or create an account with us for a quicker response.
