About Us

Non-Player Cafe is the first ever AI-driven robot cafe with old-school arcade machines. The fusion of the past and future brings a unique feel to the cafe and can be enjoyed by the young and elderly. Here at Non-Player Cafe we aim to bring joy and nostalgia to the elderly and happiness and excitement for the youth. Feel free to Book a timeslot using the booking option on the navigaiton bar.
